Factors to Consider When Making an Interstate Move

An interstate move is when individuals move from one state to another. Other names for an interstate move are long-distance moves, state-to-state moves, and cross-country moves. Regardless of what someone calls it, there are a few factors you must consider before you hire a long-distance moving company.

A local move is typically a move under 50 miles, although it varies per moving company. Many families may attempt to enlist friends to help them move only to find that not a single person is available.

What is Intrastate Moving?

An intrastate move is when an individual moves across city lines within a state. This is different from an interstate move, which is between different states.

We don’t want to minimize how difficult an intrastate move can be. That’s why we suggest still taking advantage of intrastate moving services.
